# 形式逻辑是什么

 Formal logic concerns the study of a certain kind of language which, like any language, can **serve to express states of [[affair|affairs]]**.
 形式逻辑像别的语言一样, 是一种服务于描述一种**事物的状态**的语言.
 
 It is a **formal language**.
 是一种**正式的**语言

[[entailment|Entailment]] is central because by understanding it better we can tell when some states of affairs must obtain provided some other states of [[affair|affairs]] [[obtain]]. ^4e59bd
蕴涵是核心的，因为通过更好地理解它，我们就能判断在某些**其他状态存在的情况下**，某些状态**必须存在**。
